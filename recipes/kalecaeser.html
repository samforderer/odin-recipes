Kale Caeser

Sweet potatoes are roasted to perfection with chunky onion wedges and garlic, then finished with a drizzle of balsamic vinegar for a naturally sweet holiday side dish. My Granny made this for Easter dinner. We were all sitting at the table commenting on how delicious it was and asked for the recipe.

Ingredients

  1. 4 cups chopped fresh kale
  2. 4 cups chopped Romaine lettuce
  3. 2 cups croutons
  4. 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  5. 1 batch Lime Caesar Dressing, below
  6. optional: 1 cup halved cherry or grape tomatoes

Steps

  1. Add the kale, Romaine, croutons, Parmesan, dressing, and tomatoes (if using) to a large bowl. Toss until combined.
  2. Serve immediately.
